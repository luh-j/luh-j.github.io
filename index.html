I'm a Master's student in Robotics at Johns Hopkins University, working at the intersection of AI and human expertise. My research focuses on culturally-aware foundation models and human-AI collaboration systems for high-stakes applications in sports analytics, surgical robotics, and embodied intelligence.

I graduated from UC Berkeley with triple majors in Computer Science (Honors), Economics, and Music. My work develops AI systems that enhance human expertise and augment decision-making capabilities, bridging technical innovation with real-world impact.

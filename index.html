Background

About

I'm a Master's student in Robotics at Johns Hopkins University, working across CLSP, SARG, SMARTS Lab, and Intuitive Computing Lab. I completed my undergraduate at UC Berkeley with a triple major in Computer Science, Economics, and Music.

My research vision centers on developing AI that dramatically amplifies human abilities rather than replacing them. I believe the future lies in augmentation—creating systems that enable people to see, think, and act in extraordinary ways.

Research Focus

  • Culturally-Aware Foundation Models
    Making vision-language models respect and represent diverse cultural perspectives
  • Human-AI Collaboration Systems
    Developing agentic frameworks for high-stakes environments like surgery and sports analytics

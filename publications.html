From Chaos to Clarity: AI-Driven News Intelligence for NFL Front Offices

Xiaojian Sun, Siva Indukuri, Nahuel Albayrak, Joyce Chen, Kenton Murray, Anton Dahbura, Tad Berkery
MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference (SSAC) 2026

We present a comprehensive AI system that transforms unstructured sports news into actionable intelligence for NFL front offices. Our RAG-based approach processes 40,000+ articles covering 2,000 prospects, reducing scouting research time by 65% while preserving analyst judgment.

GUIDES: Guidance Using Instructor-Distilled Embeddings for Robot Policy Enhancement

Minquan Gao, Xiaojian Sun, Qing Yan, Xinyi Li, Xiaopan Zhang, Jiachen Li, Chien-Ming Huang
IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2026

We introduce GUIDES, a framework that leverages instructor-distilled embeddings to enhance robot learning from demonstrations. Our approach achieves 40% improvement in task success rates compared to baseline methods across diverse manipulation tasks.

Adaptive Teleoperation Motion Scaling Based on Human Performance Characterization

Xiaojian Sun, Kaichen Yu, Peter Kazanzides, Adnan Munawar
IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2026

We present an adaptive teleoperation system that dynamically adjusts motion scaling based on real-time characterization of surgeon performance. Our system optimizes precision while maintaining surgeon agency in critical surgical procedures.

BatVision: Regression-Calibrated Computer Vision Pipeline for Precise Equipment Analysis

Xiaojian Sun, Junlin Wu, Anton Dahbura
MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference (SSAC) 2026

We develop BatVision, a computer vision system for precise baseball equipment measurement. Using regression-calibrated deep learning, our system achieves sub-millimeter precision in bat diameter estimation, enabling data-driven equipment optimization.
